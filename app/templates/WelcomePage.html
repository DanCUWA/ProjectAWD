Welcome to Game Master

What's this?

This is a sandbox, text-based, Role-playing game. You will be a character of your choosing, built from scratch by you. Each scenario or continuation of the story is influenced by you. An adventurous world of your choosing awaits you. A world in which you control exactly what you do, say, see and feel. Nothing is outside the realm of possibility. The one who will decide your fate from your actions is the Game Master, an AI that will give you your story based on your particular actions.
/

Lone wolf or a Comrade

You can party up with friends or play alone When alone the story continues at your pace. When in a party, don't worry about having to finish the story, the world simply awaits your response. You and your friends can decide the rate at which you play. Play all night or live your busy lives and get back to the adventure when you can.
